About Clash of Clans

Discover the amazing world of Clash of Clans

What is a Clan?

Clans are an optional feature in the game, but its strongly recommended that every player should join one.

Collaboration within a clan is essential for success.

Clans compete in two main ways:

  • Trophy Collection: Members pool their trophies and battle to collect the most trophies.
  • Clan Wars: Clans participate in wars to earn loot and Clan XP.

Within a clan, members can socialize through private chat, challenge each other, and donate troops, spells, and siege machines.

Joining a Clan

To become a member of a clan, you must first repair your Clan Castle (located in the South West of your village). There are several ways to join a clan:

  1. View a clan with the status “Anyone Can Join” and tap the “join” button.
  2. Send a request to join an “invite-only” clan.
  3. Accept an invitation sent by a clan elder, co-leader, or leader.

Some clans have minimum trophy and Town Hall requirements for joining.

Creating a New Clan

To create a new clan, you must first rebuild the Clan Castle. Access the “Create Clan” interface from the Clan button on the Clan Castle. Name your clan (between 2 and 15 characters, including spaces). You can have up to 50 members in a clan, including the leader.
