What is Clash of Clans?

Clash of Clans is a popular mobile strategy game where players build and upgrade their village, train troops, and attack other players to earn resources. The game features both single-player campaigns and multiplayer battles, and encourages players to join together in clans for mutual benefit and support.

Released in 2012 by Supercell, Clash of Clans has grown to become one of the highest-grossing mobile games of all time, thanks to its engaging gameplay, regular updates, and vibrant community.
